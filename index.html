About Us

We are a small organization that is looking to help pets in need. We were started in 2018 by 2 pet lovers that felt they needed to do something for all pets that needed a home.
We believe that every pet deserves a loving and warm home where they can be safe. With the help of more pet lovers like us, we hope we can help provide more pets with a safe and loving home. We rely mostly on donations. We offer services of providing the options to adopt pets we have found, yet made safe
